Taco

Description

A taco is a food that is of mexican origin and consists of a tortilla with a filling. The tortilla should then be doubled throughout the filling. A taco can consist of a diversity of fillings such as chicken, pork, beef, vegetables, etc. It is often embellished with several condiments, such as sour cream, guacamole, sauce and guacamole.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Ground meat
  • Chicken breast
  • Onion
  • Sauce
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Cut and cook the ground meat and the chicken breast
  2. Cut the onion in very small pieces
  3. Cook the tortilla
  4. Add the ground meat and chicken in the tortilla
  5. Add the onion in the tortilla
  6. Add sauce and mayonaise
